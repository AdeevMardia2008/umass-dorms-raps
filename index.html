What is a Z-room? A Z-room splits a traditional double into two connected sections, giving you and your roommate significantly more privacy and personal space — students describe it as "like living in a single." Only available in select Southwest halls. Northeast halls are older buildings with steam radiators, low ceilings (~7ft), and standard-sized doubles (~187 sq ft). No Z-rooms exist in Northeast. Floor plans require UMass login at umass.edu/living.
